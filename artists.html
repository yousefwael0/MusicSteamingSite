The Weeknd

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, known professionally as The Weeknd, is a Canadian singer-songwriter. Known for his distinctive voice and dark R&B style, he has won multiple Grammy Awards and created chart-topping hits like "Blinding Lights" and "Starboy".

Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish is an American singer-songwriter who gained fame with her unique style and haunting vocals. Her debut album "When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?" won multiple Grammy Awards, including Album of the Year.

Popular Songs:

Frank Ocean

Frank Ocean is an American singer-songwriter known for his innovative R&B and soul music. His critically acclaimed albums "Channel Orange" and "Blonde" have established him as one of the most influential artists of his generation.
